Profile

Paul is currently Senior Fellow and Executive Director at Verizon, leading a team of researchers and developers building new innovative communications services. Prior to joining Verizon, he worked at Microsoft Corp. and Firefly Network Inc., on web commerce, advertising, authentication, recommendations, privacy and security.
